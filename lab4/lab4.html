How to Make Coffee in a Coffee Maker:

  • Using the measurements on the side of the pot, fill the pot with the desired cups of water
  • Pour the water from the pot into the back chamber of the coffee maker
  • Place the pot back in the coffee maker
  • Open the bag of coffee beans and pour the desired amounts of beans into the coffee grinder
  • Set the grinder to the preferred grind setting (medium-coarse is recommended for a coffee maker)
  • Set the grinder’s volume measurement to the number of cups that corresponds with the amount of water filled in the pot
  • Press the button that grinds the beans
  • While the beans grind, line the permanent filter with a single-serve filter
  • Once the beans are ground, remove the container from the grinder
  • Pour the grinds into the lined filter
  • Return the container to the grinder
  • Close the lid of the coffee maker and press the “on” button
  • Wait for the coffee to brew
  • In the meantime, grab your favorite mug from the cabinet
  • Once the coffee finishes, take the pot from the coffee maker and pour the coffee into the mug
  • Drink the coffee
